SGA Overview

SGA Website ➤

SGA or Sheffield Graduate Attributes are attributes which the university community in conjunction with external partners have agreed upon that should be helpful for students' success. These attributes are in three categories; My learning, My impact and My self.

My learning skills are to help you learn and apply the course content most effectively. These include academic skills, applying knowledge, research and critacal thinking and finally digital capability.

My impact are attributes that focus on your impact on other people and society. These attributes include interpersonal skills, working with others, equality and inclution and finally ethics and sustainability.

My self are attributes that focus on your development and wellbeing to support yourself through your academic and professional career. The attributes include positive welling, purpose, personal development and enterprising.

SGA Examples

I created a time table for each day of the week which includes what module I should aim to revise in my free time. This shows my study skills.

For my group project in my Software Engineering module I volunteered to ask write up and questions to the client, this is an example of my confident Communication skills.

In my computer science class and outside of that I have made very trusting relationships, also with students who are in the year above also doing computer science and they have answered my questions about what challenges to expect this first year, this shows my healthy relationship development.

mySkills Overview

mySkills Website ➤

The mySkills is a portfolio of your skills, experiences and personal development avalible to University of Sheffield. It record and add upon this profile and this is to help you create an interesting and strong CV.

To get started you must take a 'skills assessment' which is are multiple-choice questions about some of your skills. This is a time to be honest so mySkills can help you grow in the best way. After the assessment you can select skills to develop and improve, they you can start creating a record of experiences, extra-curricular activities and life lessons.

The mySkills will help you on reflecting assignments, acheve goals and prepare you for conversations with advisers.
